The coaches of Spirit Twins Lacrosse™ have a unique teaching/playing style that is a hybrid of indoor (box) and filed lacrosse styles. This clinic will help young lacrosse players learn from both of these skill sets. Whether or not your child is looking to improve their abilities on the field or is looking to pick up box lacrosse this clinic is for them. Integrating box and field lacrosse styles increases stick handling skills, box lacrosse IQ, and developing coping skills for physical lacrosse. Additionally, players will learn how to dodge like a box lacrosse player. Coaches Clay and Kroy Arnold excel at teaching indoor (box) lacrosse skills to help students learn the box lacrosse game and/or become better field players.


A full coverage helmet, gloves, and a lacrosse stick are required.
